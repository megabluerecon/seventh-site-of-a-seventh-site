About Me

I am an emerging software developer getting my certifications at Seattle Central College. I will be starting my internship in a few short months where I will be able to apply my knowledge in HTML, CSS, Javascript, Java, C# and C++ in the real professional world. I also have background in jobs like fast food and retail where communication with customers is key. This allows me to be well-rounded when talking to people of any culture or background.

My background in technology is a unique one. I was born in Vietnam and lived eight years of my life there. My household was the only one with a computer in my entire neighborhood. This early exposure to computers is what got me latched onto technology and video games at an early age.

My first experience in coding was an intro to computer science class I took in my Sophomore year of high school. I was hooked with the concept and I sought to learn more. The following year, I took AP Computer Science class also offered at my high school. I earned a 4 grade on the AP test and created my first game at the end of that year. In college, I took various coding classes where I learned C#, C++, Java, HTML/CSS/JS.

I am a big computer enthusiast so if you are like me, follow me on LinkedIn and take a look at my GitHub!
